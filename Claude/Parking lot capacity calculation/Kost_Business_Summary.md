## 3. Why Pure Parking Fails

### The Brutal Math

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Land cost | Rp 14,490,000,000 |
| Site prep (asphalt, gate, booth, lighting) | Rp 600,000,000 |
| **Total CapEx** | **Rp 15,090,000,000** |
| Monthly revenue (84 cars × Rp 300k × 60% occupancy) | Rp 15,120,000 |
| Annual gross | Rp 181,440,000 |
| Operating cost | (Rp 90,000,000) |
| Pajak Parkir 25% | (Rp 45,360,000) |
| **Annual net** | **Rp 46,080,000** |
| **Payback period** | **~327 years** 🪦 |
| **ROI** | **<0.5%/year** |

### Why It Fails
- Rp 300,000/month per car is too low for Rp 14.5B land value
- Cars-only model misses motorcycle demand (90% of student transport)
- Faculty park free at campus → very low actual demand
- A bank deposit gives 5-6% — vastly better with zero risk

## 4. Business Model Comparison

| Model | Investment | Net/Month | BEP | Total Return |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pure car parking | Rp 15B | Rp 3.8M | 327 yrs | ❌ Disaster |
| Motorcycle + retail | Rp 17B | Rp 71M | 20 yrs | ✅ 5%+ |
| **Kost + commercial** | **Rp 20B** | **Rp 107M** | **15.5 yrs** | **✅ 8-12%** |

### Why Kost Wins for PTN Location

State universities (PTN) are the **best possible scenario** for kost because:
- **Volume**: 20,000-50,000+ students vs 3,000-10,000 at private
- **Stability**: Cheaper tuition = students from all over Indonesia = they NEED kost
- **Predictable cycles**: New batch every August/September, year-round stable occupancy
- **Mid-price sweet spot**: Rp 1.2-2M/month consistent demand

Real examples: UI Depok, UGM Yogya, ITB Bandung, Unair Surabaya, Unpad Jatinangor — all created multi-billion rupiah kost empires.

## 7. Financing Strategy

### Don't Pay Cash — Use Leverage

| Aspect | Cash Purchase | 60% Mortgage |
|---|---|---|
| Initial cash out | Rp 14.5B | Rp 5.8B |
| Liquidity remaining | Low | Rp 8.7B available |
| Tax deduction | None | Interest fully deductible |
| Effective land cost | 100% | ~70% after tax savings |
| **ROI on equity** | **~6.5%** | **~12-14%** |

### Recommended Structure (Conservative)

- **Land mortgage**: 60% LTV = Rp 8.7B @ 10% (15-year tenor)
- **Equity for land**: Rp 5.8B
- **Construction loan**: Rp 4B @ 11% (10-year, milestone drawdowns)
- **Equity for build/working capital**: Rp 2B
- **Total equity invested**: Rp 7.8B
- **Total debt**: Rp 12.7B
- **Monthly debt service**: ~Rp 145 juta

### Bank Options (Indonesia 2026)
- **BCA** — Best rates, strict documentation
- **Mandiri** — Flexible income proof, slightly higher rate
- **BTN** — Property investor specialty, easier underwriting
- **BSI** — Sharia option, sometimes cheaper effective rate

> **Tip**: Get quotes from 3-4 banks. A 1-2% rate spread = Rp 100M+ saved over loan life.

## 8. Legal & Tax Structure

### Use PT (Perseroan Terbatas) — NOT Personal Name

**Why**:
- Liability protection
- Tax efficiency (22% corporate vs up to 35% personal)
- Easier inheritance/transfer (shares vs land titles)
- Better bank financing access
- Future exit through share sale (lower tax)

### PT Setup (4-6 weeks, ~Rp 5 juta)

| Step | Detail |
|---|---|
| 1. Choose name | Check available via AHU online |
| 2. Akta Pendirian | Via notaris |
| 3. SK Kemenkumham | Legal recognition |
| 4. NPWP perusahaan | Tax ID |
| 5. NIB (OSS) | Online business ID |
| 6. SIUP/TDP | Bundled in NIB |
| 7. Business bank account | Open with notaris docs |

**KBLI to register**: 55130 (Akomodasi) + 68111 (Real estat) + 47111 (commercial rental)

### Permits — PBG (~Rp 295 juta, 6-8 months)

| Permit | Cost (Rp) |
|---|---|
| Soil & survey | 15,000,000 |
| Design package | 120,000,000 |
| UKL-UPL environmental | 35,000,000 |
| PBG application | 60,000,000 |
| SLF post-build | 25,000,000 |
| Consultant fees | 40,000,000 |

### Tax Strategy — Phased Approach

**Phase 1 (Year 1-4): UMKM PP 23/2018**
- Tax rate: **0.5% final on gross turnover**
- Eligibility: Annual turnover under Rp 4.8B
- Annual tax: only Rp 5-8 juta

**Phase 2 (Year 4+): Standard Corporate**
- Tax rate: 22% on net profit
- Key deductions: Building depreciation (5%/yr), Equipment (25%/yr), Mortgage interest (100%), All operating costs
- **Effective rate stays at 2-4% of gross revenue** through proper depreciation

### Other Taxes

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **PBB** (annual property) | 0.1-0.3% of NJOP | Rp 15-25 juta/year |
| **BPHTB** (one-time at purchase) | 5% of NJOP | ~Rp 700M one-time |
| **PPN** (VAT) | 12% (eff. 11%) | Triggered at Rp 4.8B turnover |
| **PPh Final 10%** | On commercial rent | Tenants withhold and remit |
| **Pajak Hotel/Restoran** | N/A | Kost exempted under PP 35/2023 |

> **Hire a tax consultant**: Rp 3-5 juta/month for proper bookkeeping. Saves 5-10x in mistakes.

## 9. Construction Plan

### Building Specifications

**Site & Structural**:
- Footprint: 1,200 m² (20m × 60m)
- 3 floors, no basement, ~3,000 m² built area
- Foundation: Foot plate / strauss pile (after soil test)
- Structure: RC frame, beton K-250 minimum
- Walls: Hebel preferred (better than bata merah)
- Roof: Baja ringan + genteng metal
- Ceiling height: 2.8m minimum

**Standard Room (40 units)**:
- 3m × 4m = 12 m²
- Private bathroom 1.8 m²
- Granite tile flooring
- Bed + mattress (Royal Foam 20cm), wardrobe, desk, chair, fan
- 4+ electrical outlets

**Eksklusif Room (10 units)**:
- 3.5m × 5m = 17.5 m²
- AC 1 PK (Daikin/Panasonic only)
- Water heater
- Mini fridge optional
- Better mattress (Comforta/Olympic)

### Realistic Budget (Rp 5.03B with contingency)

| Category | Cost (Rp) |
|---|---|
| Soil test + Design | 135,000,000 |
| Permits (IMB/PBG) | 85,000,000 |
| Site prep + Foundation | 450,000,000 |
| Structure (RC + walls) | 1,900,000,000 |
| Roofing + Flooring | 500,000,000 |
| MEP (plumbing + electrical) | 660,000,000 |
| Doors, windows, painting | 390,000,000 |
| Furniture (50 rooms) | 300,000,000 |
| AC + water heater (10 eksklusif) | 115,000,000 |
| Common areas | 180,000,000 |
| CCTV + Security | 45,000,000 |
| Wifi infrastructure | 25,000,000 |
| Landscaping + signage | 55,000,000 |
| Contingency (5%) | 190,000,000 |
| **TOTAL** | **5,028,000,000** |

### Contractor Selection Process

**Step 1: Shortlist (2 weeks)**
- 5-7 candidates from architect referrals + physical site visits
- Reject any without: NPWP, NIB, SIUJK license, 5+ year track record

**Step 2: Payment Schedule (CRITICAL)**

| Milestone | % Payment |
|---|---|
| Contract signing | 10% |
| Foundation complete | 15% |
| Ground floor structure | 15% |
| 2nd floor structure | 15% |
| 3rd floor + roof | 15% |
| Walls + plaster | 10% |
| MEP + finishing | 15% |
| Handover + 1 month retention | 5% |

**Red Flags to Walk Away From**:
- 🚩 Wants >20% upfront (fraud risk)
- 🚩 Quotes 30%+ below others (will cut rebar/concrete corners)
- 🚩 No clear BOQ breakdown (will add "extra work" invoices)

**Quality Control (Mandatory)**:
- Hire INDEPENDENT site supervisor (Rp 6-8 juta/month)
- Concrete test at every pour (independent lab)
- Daily photo documentation
- Weekly progress meetings
- Hold 5% retention for 3-6 months post-handover

### Phased Build Strategy

**Phase 1 (Months 1-5)**: Commercial strip first
- Smaller scope, tests contractor before big build
- Start generating Rp 35 juta/month from month 6

**Phase 2 (Months 6-18)**: Kost building
- Open August Year 2 to catch new student batch

### Property Manager Profile

**Background**: Hospitality diploma OR 3+ years kost management experience
**Age**: 28-45
**Key Skills**: Basic accounting, Excel, conflict mediation, basic maintenance
**Critical Traits**: Honest (handles cash), patient with students, strict on rules
**KPI Bonus**: Rp 500k/month per percentage point above 90% occupancy

### Manager's Responsibilities
- Tenant onboarding (KTP verification, contracts, deposits)
- Rent collection (digital preferred)
- Marketing on Mamikos, Cove, OLX (monthly updates)
- Maintenance coordination
- Tenant complaint mediation
- Monthly P&L reports with photos to owner

### Tenant Screening (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- KTP photo + holder verification
- Surat orang tua/wali (parental consent)
- Verified parent/guardian phone number
- Proof of student status (KTM) or work (slip gaji)
- Deposit 1 month + rent 1 month upfront
- Signed contract (notarised for 6+ months)

### House Rules
- No overnight guests of opposite sex
- Quiet hours 10pm-6am
- No smoking inside (designated area only)
- No drugs/alcohol = immediate eviction
- Visitors register at booth, depart by 10pm
- 3 late payments = eviction

### Eviction Protocol
1. Day 1 late: WhatsApp reminder
2. Day 7: Phone to tenant + parent
3. Day 14: SP 1 (formal notice)
4. Day 21: SP 2 (termination warning)
5. Day 30: SP 3 (eviction notice, deposit forfeit)
6. Day 45: Physical eviction with RT/RW witnesses, belongings stored 30 days

### Operations Calendar

**Daily**: Security check, CCTV review, walkthrough, inquiry responses, occupancy tracking
**Weekly**: 10 random room inspections, pest control walk, payment reminders, social media content
**Monthly**: P&L report, marketing performance review, tenant satisfaction informal survey
**Quarterly**: Deep clean, professional pest control, pricing adjustment, owner site visit
**Annually**: Full building inspection, repaint, equipment depreciation review, insurance renewal

## 14. Key Lessons & Final Wisdom

### The Three Cardinal Rules

**1. Location > Building > Operations > Marketing**
If location is wrong, nothing fixes it. If building is bad, ops and marketing can't save you. If ops are weak, marketing wastes money. Get the order right.

**2. Cash Flow Beats Net Worth**
A Rp 30B building with empty rooms is poorer than a Rp 5B building at 95% occupancy. Optimize for cash flow first 5 years, asset value emerges naturally.

**3. The Boring Stuff Makes the Money**
Sexy stuff: design, branding, opening party. Boring stuff: payment collection, maintenance schedule, tenant screening, monthly reporting. **The boring stuff is 95% of your returns.**

### Three Mistakes That Destroy This Business

1. **Hiring a relative as manager** — they steal or neglect; family disputes ruin both relationships and business
2. **Cutting construction quality 10-15% to save money** — you pay 3x to fix it within 5 years
3. **Skipping the tax consultant** — a Rp 50 juta consultant saves Rp 500 juta in mistakes

### Three Decisions That Win

1. **Don't pay cash for the land** — 60% mortgage keeps liquidity, generates leveraged returns
2. **Build commercial strip first** — faster cash flow, smaller risk, tests contractor
3. **Property manager is the most important hire** — Rp 8jt/month + KPI bonus, interview 5+ candidates

### The Bottom Line

From **Rp 7.8B equity invested** → **Rp 35.5B total wealth in 10 years** (4.5x return)

Annual cash flow stabilizes at **Rp 1.2-1.7B by Year 5+**

You're not buying parking. You're building a multi-decade asset that:
- Throws off cash monthly
- Appreciates 7%/year automatically
- Has 40% revenue diversification (kost + commercial)
- Can be expanded vertically or sold as a business unit
