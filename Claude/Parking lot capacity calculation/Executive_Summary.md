# Executive Summary — Land Investment Decision

**Land**: 20m × 115m (2,300 m²) | **Cost**: Rp 14.49B | **Location**: Near PTN + Residential

---

## The Question

> "Can I build a parking lot business on this land at Rp 300k/car/month?"

## The Honest Answer

**No.** Pure parking is mathematically impossible to profit from on this land value.

| Pure Parking Numbers |  |
|---|---|
| Investment | Rp 15B |
| Monthly net | Rp 3.8M |
| Payback | **327 years** |
| ROI | <0.5%/year |

## The Better Answer

**Build a Kost (boarding house) + Commercial strip instead.**

The location near a state university (PTN) is the best possible scenario for kost business — high demand, stable occupancy, predictable cycles.

## The 3 Critical Decisions

### 1. Don't Pay Cash for Land
Use 60% mortgage. Keeps Rp 8.7B liquid for construction + buffer. Mortgage interest is fully tax-deductible. ROI on equity jumps from 6.5% → 12-14%.

### 2. Build Commercial Strip First
- Smaller scope (Rp 800M vs Rp 5B kost)
- Faster cash flow (open in 5 months)
- Tests contractor before betting big
- Generates Rp 35M/month to fund kost construction

### 3. Hire a Property Manager
- Budget: Rp 8M/month + KPI bonuses
- Interview 5+ candidates
- This single hire determines success or failure
- Never hire family for this role

## What NOT to Do

❌ Build pure parking lot
❌ Pay cash for land
❌ Skip soil test (Rp 15jt saves Rp 200jt of mistakes)
❌ Use cheapest contractor (will cut concrete/rebar grade)
❌ Pay >20% upfront to contractor
❌ Hire relative as manager
❌ Cut construction budget by 10-15% to save
❌ Skip tax consultant
❌ Build standard rooms below 12 m²
❌ Use cheap AC brands in eksklusif rooms
